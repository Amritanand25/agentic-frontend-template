# S.M.A.R.T. Goals Framework for UX Projects

> **SMART is an acronym giving criteria to guide in the setting of objectives. In UX design SMART goals enable to clarify ideas, focus efforts and estimate time and resources efficiently.**

S.M.A.R.T. goals framework is a great technique that allows you to define clear, specific, and measurable goals and potential outcomes in your UX projects. This methodology can be applied to different domain areas, and for UX design it's vital for understanding ROI and impact of design.

---

## The Framework

### **S** -- Specific

Define exactly what you want to accomplish.

> _Example: With the new design we are planning on increasing lead conversion by 20% by the end of the next quarter._

### **M** -- Measurable

Establish concrete criteria for measuring progress.

> _Example: We can measure the increase of conversions by tracking the number of page views and unique visitors versus the clicks on the primary CTA._

### **A** -- Actionable

Ensure the goal can be broken down into concrete steps.

> _Example: Because the goal is specific, we can define what combination of content, functionality and visual hierarchy will enhance UX and drive conversion._

### **R** -- Relevant

Align the goal with broader business objectives.

> _Example: This specific goal is relevant to a higher business objective: increase total sales revenue and drive users adoption._

### **T** -- Trackable

Define how progress will be monitored over time.

> _Example: We can track the conversion rate and overall number of sales over an extended period of time._

---

## Practical Tips

For each part of S.M.A.R.T., ask the following questions:

### Specific

- What do I want to accomplish?
- Why is this goal important?
- Who is involved?
- Where is it located?
- Which resources or limits are involved?
- When?
- What can I do today?

### Measurable

- How much?
- How many?
- How will I know when it's accomplished?

### Actionable

- How can I accomplish the goal?
- How realistic is the goal?

### Relevant

- Does it seem worthwhile?
- Is this the right time?
- Does this match our other efforts/needs?
- Am I the right person to reach this goal?

### Trackable

- How will I track progress?
